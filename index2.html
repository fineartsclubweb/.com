Fine Arts Club

Fine Arts Club,commonly called FAC,is the embodiement of the delicate talent of arts and crafts and is one of the most prestigious clubs in the college. We thrive to enhance the art culture in the campus and beyond it. Through our distinctive and enchanting activities each year, we emphasize on creating a vibrant and colourful atmosphere on the campus. We provide equal oppurtunities to every curious and interested student to colorize their point of view and to portray their skills.

The fine arts organizes various art events in the campus. We raise our helping hands in different cultural and technical events in the college. We help in decorations,making posters etc. Club members will get to volunteer in these events. From this year on, we are going to organize a workshop to help students showcase their talent providing them a strong platform and later certifying their talents

The principles of true art is not to portray, but to evoke
